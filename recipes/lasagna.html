Lasagna Delight

Description

Who needs a mother when you've got the internet to provide such high quality lasagna?

Just read the below Ingredients and Steps to make your own mouth-watering lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Parmesan
  • Ground beef
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano
  • Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 deg F.
  2. Bring pot of salted water to a boil. When boiling, add in lasagna sheets. Cook slightly less than al dente, then drain, cool with cold water, set aside.
  3. Heat a generous amount of olive oil on a pan on medium. Once hot, add finely diced garlic, organo, basil, ground beef, onion and cook for 5 minutes. Add celery, carrots for 2-3 minutes. Add salt, pepper, tomato sauce, and simmer for 10 minutes. Reduce heat to low.
  4. Line deep sheet pan with foil. Prepare the lasagna dish. Do the following 3 times:
    1. Spread some sauce on the pan.
    2. Layer lasagna sheets on top.
    3. Layer mozzarella, ricotta, parmesan on top.
  5. Cook in the oven for 30 minutes. Pull out of oven, let sit for 5-10 minutes until cooled a bit, then serve.
  6. Enjoy.
